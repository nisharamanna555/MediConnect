What is MediConnect?

MediConnect is a project to improve communication between patients, physicians, and pharmacists. Users can easily share their medical records with each other. Medical professionals can access to patients' records written from other hospoitals. With MediConnect doctors can reduce risks of misdiagnosis and patients can have quicker but safter prescriptions. Make a MediConnect account and try easy-to-go features for your prescriptions!
